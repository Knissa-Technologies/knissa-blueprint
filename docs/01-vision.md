# 🌍 Knissa Vision

> **Making finance simple, secure and accessible for everyone.**

---

# Why Knissa Exists

Financial services should empower people, not create barriers.

Across Haiti and many Caribbean countries, millions of individuals and small businesses still face significant challenges when accessing modern financial services.

Some of the most common problems include:

- Limited access to banking services.
- High costs for domestic and international money transfers.
- Low adoption of digital payments.
- Limited financial tools for small businesses.
- Fragmented financial ecosystems.
- Lack of modern financial infrastructure.

Knissa exists to solve these problems.

Our mission is to build a financial platform that is simple, secure and accessible to everyone.

---

# Our Purpose

Knissa is more than a digital wallet.

We are building financial infrastructure that enables people and businesses to move, receive and manage money with confidence.

Every feature we develop must contribute to financial inclusion, transparency and trust.
