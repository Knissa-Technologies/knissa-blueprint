# 💼 Business Model

> **Building financial infrastructure for Haiti and the Caribbean.**

---

# Executive Summary

Knissa is a financial technology company focused on providing secure, modern and accessible financial services.

Our platform is designed to simplify digital payments, money transfers and financial management for individuals, merchants and businesses.

---

# Problem Statement

Millions of people across Haiti still face barriers when accessing modern financial services.

These barriers include:

- Limited banking access
- High remittance costs
- Low digital payment adoption
- Limited financial tools for small businesses
- Lack of accessible financial infrastructure

Knissa exists to solve these challenges.
